What is WWMC?

WWMC is a math competition hosted by our founders and volunteers. The competition features multiple rounds, including team and individual, which consist of intense problem solving ranging from complex algebra, to basic geometry. Not only is it filled with math fun, but prizes are on the line for individual and team round winners! This competition was designed to challenge and engage students from Edison Middle Schools in mathematical problem-solving and critical thinking. It also provides a platform for students to showcase their mathematical abilities beyond the classroom and to compete with peers from different schools.

Registration

If you'd like to register for WWMC, follow the guide below.

Learn about the competition:

Read through our website and familiarize yourself with the competition and its style.

Form a team:

Gather a team of 3-4 people, or sign up by yourself or with one other person and have us pair you with other incomplete teams.

Sign up through our form:

One person from your group should fill out a form with everyone's correct information. You can find it in the dropdown menu of the Register tab on the top.

Pay for competition fees:

The same person that filled out the form should pay for the team's registration fees below via Venmo or Zelle ($10 per person). You can find more about this in the dropdown menu of the Register tab on the top.

FAQ

Have a question? Look for the answer here!

  • Who can participate in the competition?

    All members in a participating team must attend one of Edison's public middle schools. School ID's will be needed on the day of the competition for proof.

  • When is WWMC?

    WWMC will take place on Sunday, January 12th, 2025, at Woodrow Wilson Middle School. See the section above for the schedule along with the windows for payment and registration.

  • How do I register?

    See the Registration section on our website for a step-by-step guide on that! We have a limited capacity, so register quick!

  • Can I register solo or with one other person?

    You'll have to be in a team of 3-4 people in order to participate in the competition. However, you can register individually or as a duo and have us pair you with others to form a 3-4 person team.

  • What happens if I miss the registration window?

    No matter how late, once the registration window closes, teams will not be able to register for the competition.

  • Where can I form teams?

    You can form teams with other Edison middle schoolers, or join our Discord server in the Contact section to form teams with other competitors! If you'd like, you can also register individually or with one other person for us to match you up with others in order to form a 3-4 person team.

  • Are there prizes?

    In past competitions, participants have won over $500 in cash and prizes across both individual and team rounds. This yearâ€™s prizes will be revealed at the event.

  • Can our team members vary in schools/grades?

    Your team is allowed to have members of different schools and grades, as long as they all attend one of Edison's public middle schools.

  • How does the pairing process work?

    Registration pairing is only for those who register individually or as a duo in order to get paired with others to form a 3-4 person team so they can compete. Once a solo or duo fills out the registration form and pays for their fees accordingly, they will be matched up with others to form a proper team. An email will be sent to all members letting them know they've been matched up.

  • How do I know if my team is registered for WWMC?

    To find out if your team's registered, go to the dropdown menu on the Register tab and select the Team List. Scroll through the list of teams to see if your team is on there to compete in WWMC.
